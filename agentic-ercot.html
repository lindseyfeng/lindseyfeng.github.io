Agentic ERCOT
Two LLM agents trading the Texas (ERCOT) electricity market for HB_NORTH. Both run daily at 12:30 PM CT, after the day-ahead market clears.
The Day-Ahead Forecast agent predicts tomorrow's 24 hourly DAM clearing prices. The DART Trading agent predicts tomorrow's RT−DAM spread and sizes hourly long/short positions accordingly. Both are scored daily against realized prices, and both are benchmarked against a "no-skill" persistence baseline (assume tomorrow looks like yesterday).
