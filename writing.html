Overview

Currently I am interested in exploring:

  • the boundary of words
  • narrative structure (circular, fragmented)
  • Existential themes (individual feeling alienated from his/her perception of the human reality)
I am still organizing pieces written over the past few years and actively revising them in spare time. Writing is not of any academic pursuit but just a fun thing to do in general.

Please email me at lindseyfthegreatest@gmail.com if you feel like providing any general comments/suggestion to any of my works. (I would really love them)

Playing with Words

I am into exploring the boundary of words through its spelling, sound, and meaning in different context. Specifically i am into the idea of taking words out of its contexts and playing with it
